Problem statement: Student parking

Students have trouble finding unoccupied parking spaces and don't know where to park at certain times of the day without getting into trouble. An app to help display the best times to park and which parking spots are most conventient would be extremely helpful to those that have difficulties.

Affinity Diagram: USC Student Parking App

There are many ways to implement this app, and it all starts with planning. From app architecture to user interaction/experience, our affinty diagram takes into account everything in order to create something cohesive and applicable to helping someone who needs assistance in getting around USC's terrible existing parking system.
